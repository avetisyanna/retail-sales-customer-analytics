# Business Questions

This project is focused on retail sales performance and customer behavior analysis.

## Sales Analysis Dashboard

The sales dashboard helps answer:

- Are sales increasing or decreasing over time?
- What is the total revenue, number of orders, number of customers, and average invoice value?
- Which countries generate the highest sales?
- Which products contribute most to revenue?
- Which weekdays and hours show stronger purchasing activity?
- How do current period results compare with the previous period?

## Customer Analysis Dashboard

The customer dashboard helps answer:

- Who are the most valuable customers?
- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
- How frequently do customers purchase?
- Which customers show signs of inactivity?
- Which customer groups should be targeted with marketing campaigns?
- How strong is customer retention over time?
- Which behaviors indicate loyalty?
